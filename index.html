Story Concept

Sunny ‚Äî a cheerful solar flare ‚Äî travels through space and meets people affected by its journey: farmers, pilots and astronauts. The narrative shows how space weather can affect GPS, communications, power systems and the safety of space travelers.

Story features

  • Told from the solar flare's perspective.
  • Simple, child-friendly language with colorful illustration ideas.
  • Incorporates fun facts and real-world scenarios to spark curiosity.
  • Encourages empathy, safety awareness and interest in space science.
